The Book of Acts

The book of Acts is an exciting journey through the eyes of the early church fathers.

The story begins in Jerusalem with Peter and the other apostles being baptized in the Holy Spirit and preaching to the crowds, converting many.

The reader follows as the apostles move outward. We first follow Peter, then Philip, and finally the apostle Paul.

Told by the beloved physician Luke, Acts is a real page turner!


In this study, we are using Dr. Sieffert's four-part, verse-by-verse commentary on Acts.
